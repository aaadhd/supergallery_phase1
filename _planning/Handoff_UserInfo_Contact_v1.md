## 1) 문의하기 (USR-INF-07) 적용 키

- 제목/리드

  - `contact.leadBeforeFaq`
  - `contact.leadAfterFaq`

- 필드

  - `contact.email`
  - `contact.category`
  - `contact.message`
  - `contact.placeholderName`
  - `contact.placeholderEmail`
  - `contact.categoryPlaceholder`
  - `contact.placeholderMessage`

- 카테고리

  - `contact.categoryUpload`
  - `contact.categoryReport`
  - `contact.categoryPrivacy`
  - `contact.categorySuggestion`
  - `contact.categoryBug`
  - `contact.categoryOther`

- 첨부/안내/버튼

  - `contact.attachHint`

  - `contact.submit`
  - `contact.submitting`
